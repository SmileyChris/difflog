Service Description

diff·log is a tool that generates personalized developer ecosystem summaries using the Anthropic Claude API. The service aggregates publicly available information from various developer-focused sources and uses AI to create curated summaries based on your preferences.

API Usage & Costs

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

You provide your own Anthropic API key. You are solely responsible for:

  • Keeping your API key secure
  • All charges incurred through your API key usage
  • Compliance with Anthropic's terms of service

diff·log does not have access to your API key — it's stored only in your browser and calls are made directly to Anthropic.

Creds (Prepaid Credits)

Alternatively, you can purchase creds to generate diffs without your own API key. By purchasing creds:

  • You receive the number of credits indicated at time of purchase
  • Each diff generation consumes 1 cred
  • Creds are non-refundable once used
  • Unused creds do not expire
  • Creds are tied to your email address and cannot be transferred

Payments are processed securely via Stripe. We do not store your payment details.
